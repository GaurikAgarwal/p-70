G. D. Goenka Public School, often informally referred to simply as Goenka, is a private school located in New Delhi, India. It was started by the Gayatri Devi Goenka Group in 1994. The group runs over 40 educational institutions throughout India, from pre-school to post-graduate institutes.

WORK

In 1994 the group set up its first school - GD Goenka Public School, Vasant Kunj, with 2300 students in its first year. Now the school has more than 350000 students. The school has been recognized by the Directorate of Education Delhi and affiliated to the CBSE. The faculty on board has the Heads of academic management from schools and universities. The school has 43 branches.
